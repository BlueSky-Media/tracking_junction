We're seeing a duplicate counting issue in the TrackingJunction aggregate stats view for step 6 "Quote Form" on our seniors quote page.

What we're seeing:

The aggregate view for step 6 "Quote Form" shows a badge of "2" and "RESPONSES (5 TOTAL)" with two separate rows both labeled "submitted" â€” one with count 4 (80%) and one with count 1 (20%).

What we expect:

The aggregate view should show a single "submitted" row combining all responses, not two separate "submitted" rows.
What we suspect:

We've since fixed the code to only fire one form_complete event for step 6. If old records with step_complete type for step 6 still exist in the database, they could be creating the second "submitted" row in aggregates.
